# Mório

<p> <strong>Mório</strong> is word derived from Greek work that's mean <strong>Molecules</strong> .

In interfaces, <strong>molecules</strong> are relatively simple groups of UI elements functioning together as a unit. For example, a form label, search input, and button can join together to create a search form molecule.

</p>
